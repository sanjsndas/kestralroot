🌤️ your cookie preferences

🕊️ opt‑out / third‑clouds

We partner with a small, local analytics service (RootCounter) that does not sell or share your data. You can also disable specific cookies via your browser. Some pages may embed sky maps from openstreetmap.org – they set a temporary cookie to remember map tiles. If you'd like to fully opt out of any non‑essential cookie, please use our preference centre (reachable by sky‑packet) or email privacy@kestralroot.lab.

🌿 do not track (DNT): we honor DNT signals from most modern browsers. If DNT is enabled, we only place essential roots cookies.

🧪 experimental plot cookies — from time to time we run trials (e.g. which sky‑letter layout you prefer). Those are always anonymous and you can withdraw via the toggle above.
